About Seattle Junk Removal

Seattle Junk Removal is a locally-owned and operated company serving Seattle and the greater King County area. We pride ourselves on professional, reliable service with a commitment to environmental responsibility.

Our experienced team handles jobs of all sizes, from single-item pickups to complete property cleanouts. We work with both residential and commercial clients, offering competitive rates and free estimates.

We believe in keeping Seattle clean while minimizing landfill waste through recycling, donation, and proper disposal practices.
